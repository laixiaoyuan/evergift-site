Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 17, 2026

EverGift ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, including:

  • Names, birthdays, and relationship details of people you add to the app
  • Gift ideas, preferences, and notes you enter
  • Account information if you create an account
  • Purchase information when you subscribe to EverGift Pro

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and improve the EverGift app and its features
  • Generate AI-powered gift ideas based on the preferences you provide
  • Send reminders for upcoming events and gift deadlines
  • Process subscription payments
  • Respond to your questions and support requests

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely. Personal information you enter about your contacts is stored on your device and, if you are signed in, synced to our secure servers. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • RevenueCat – for managing in-app subscriptions
  • Apple App Store – for payment processing
  • Anthropic – for AI-powered gift idea generation

These services have their own privacy policies and data practices.

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you with our services. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

Children's Privacy

EverGift is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information. To exercise these rights, please contact us at the email below.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page.
