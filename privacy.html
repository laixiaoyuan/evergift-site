Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 17, 2026

EverGift ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your information is handled when you use our mobile application.

Data Storage

EverGift does not operate its own servers or backend. All personal information you enter — including contact names, birthdays, gift ideas, preferences, and notes — is stored locally on your device using your device's local storage. EverGift does not require an account or login, and there is no cloud sync. We do not collect, transmit, or store this information on our own servers.

AI-Generated Gift Ideas and Buy Timing

When you use the AI gift idea or buy timing features, the preferences, tags, and budget information you have entered are sent to OpenAI's API for processing. This data is handled under OpenAI's Privacy Policy. EverGift does not store a server-side copy of this data. Generated results are cached locally on your device until you delete the person or clear app data.

Subscriptions

EverGift offers an optional Pro subscription. Subscription management and receipt validation are handled by RevenueCat. Payment processing is handled by Apple App Store. We do not collect or store your payment information directly. Please refer to RevenueCat's Privacy Policy and Apple's Privacy Policy for details on how they handle your data.

Third-Party Services

EverGift uses the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • OpenAI – AI-powered gift idea generation and buy timing suggestions
  • RevenueCat – subscription management and receipt validation
  • Apple App Store – payment processing

Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect, store, or sell any personal information on our own servers. We do not use analytics tools, advertising SDKs, or tracking technologies.

Children's Privacy

EverGift is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by updating the date at the top of this page.
